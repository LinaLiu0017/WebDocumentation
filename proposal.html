Background

The arctic is warming at almost twice the rate of global average due to climate change (Marine Mammal Commission, 2020). The arctic animals rely on the unique and special arctic environment, the climate change affect the arctic species. Based on statistic, Sea ice in the North Pole is decreasing by 13.1 percent on September per decade (Earth Science Communications Team, 2020). Therefore, the living environment of Arctic animals shrink to new lows. The serious decline in connectivity, extent and sea ice indicates the disappearance of functional habitat (USGS, 2014). The reduction of sea ices cause the decreasing foraging opportunities for polar bears. The polar bears have less reproduction and smaller body size that also directly connect to the less arctic sea.

Research

The goal of the website aims to raise fund to protect arctic animal and their ecosystem. Thus, the website introduces the climate warming that negative affect our ecosystem and arctic animal. These provided information raise people concern on climate and environmental changes. Its appeals and educates people to protect and save our planet. World Wildlife Fund (WWF) has many experiences on securing polar bears since 1972 (WWF, n.d.). WWF can be my indicator and reference by updating information of arctic animals and developing website quality.

User Profiles

The target audient is people who can well afford to donate and protect arctic animals. Besides, the donator is not limited to individual person because the project requires more financial support. The charity activity on website can collaborate with certain organizations or companies to raise funds for the website. For example, the website promotes an organisation or company’s advertisement if they kindly provide financial support.

The design of website is simple and concise, because the audience basically includes everyone. The requirement of design is that everyone easily understand what the website about and simply make a donation.

Ideation

The concept is a charity website that helps to protect the arctic environment and save the arctic animals. We offer different volunteer projects for people to join. The stories of what happening in arcticle is also a important part to show people, the floating ice in arctic is melting, polar bears are losing their home and ocean carbage is impact the environment in the arctic. Users can simply join as voluteer project to make a contribution to solve those issues by contact us. Users can also donate money to our organization to help us keep doing our projects.
